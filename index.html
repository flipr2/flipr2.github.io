Why Choose FLIPR 2.0?

Infrared Thermometer

FLIPR 2.0 allows you to measure body temperature upto 10 cm range with more than 99.9% accuracy. Our unique alarm and optical indication based technology shows exactly when the temperature is above the average human temperature to indicate a potential candidate of Virus. It operates completely touchlessly So that you never come in contact with a person having high temperature. It comes fitted with high quality Ultrasonic Sensors so that it operates in both indoors and outdoors.

Automatic Hand Sanitizer Machine

FLIPR 2.0 allows you to sanitize hands touchlessly. It offers World-class ABS material for protection and longer durability and the system is completely waterproof. It comes with a lid on the tank which makes the Sanitizer stay longer and prevents it from evaporating. The tank has a liquid filling arrangement and the machine is completely leakage free. There is an indicator lamp for Automatic Hand Detection below the machine which helps the user operate the machine completely touchless and helps in Zero Wastage. It comes with electric adapter and screws for wall-mounting the machine.
